Breaches from unknown sources

If you suspect a breach from an unknown source on your device, a report can be made to the Australian Cybercrime Online Reporting Network (ACORN). Complaints they can deal with include: hacking, scams, fraud, identity theft, attacks on computer systems (including unauthorised access) and illegal or prohibited online content.

If relevant, you may be contacted by a law enforcement agency to further investigate your issue.
